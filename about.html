About MALIBA SECONDARY SCHOOL

Discover our history, mission, and values

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that develops students academically, physically, socially, and morally, preparing them to become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, recognized for producing well-rounded individuals who excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society.

Our History

Founded in 1975, MALIBA SECONDARY SCHOOL has grown to become one of the most prestigious educational institutions in the region. With over four decades of experience, we have successfully educated thousands of students who have gone on to achieve great success in various fields.

Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education and character development.

Integrity

We believe in honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our dealings.

Community

We foster a supportive community where every individual is valued and respected.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and technologies to enhance learning experiences.
